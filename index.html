Future Home of Singed Feathers

A vigilante, an arsonist and pigeons. A game where two players’ wits are clashed against each other. Singed Feathers, a strategy game where you fight for the fate of pigeons. Become a master strategist and be matched against willing opponents, or invite a friend to their demise. Be creative and design a playing field to your advantage. Share your meticulous creations with others, or take the challenge of owning up on a field designed by your enemy. But before you begin there’s one question: Save the pigeons or burn them? What side do you choose?
